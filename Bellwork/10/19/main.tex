	\[f'(x) = 3x^2 + 2xe^x + x^2e^x\]
	\vfill
	\[\text{Point-Slope Form: }y-f(-1)=f'(-1)(x+1)\]
	\[y = \left(3-\frac{2}{e}+\frac{1}{e}\right)(x+1)+\left(-1+\frac{1}{e}\right)\]
	\[\boxed{y = \left(\frac{3e-1}{e}\right)x+2}\] % *: "Rise-Over-Run" format for viewing the slope.
